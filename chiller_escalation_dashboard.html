Escalation ladder

1Pump ramps up — more flow to carry the heat, holds ΔT
2Chiller loads up — compressor works harder to hold CHWS
3Tower fans ramp — reject extra heat, fight rising lift
4Stage up a chiller — capacity limit reached
5Loss of control — CHWS drifts above setpoint, comfort slips

Hit Occupancy spike and watch the ladder climb. Toggle Predictive on, reset, and spike again — the plant pre-stages before the load arrives, so CHWS barely moves. That gap between reactive and predictive is the core value of AI optimization.
